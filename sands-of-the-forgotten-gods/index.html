J. R. Caldera

Archaeological & mythological thriller author

J.R. Caldera is a storyteller with a deep passion for ancient civilizations, forgotten knowledge, and the thrilling question of what happens when the past refuses to stay buried.

With Sands of the Forgotten Gods, he turns from buried conspiracies to buried gods — asking what it would really mean if the myths of Ancient Egypt were never metaphors, but memories of something that is still, quietly, waiting.

He loves connecting with readers and always appreciates hearing which book — or which god — unsettled you most.
