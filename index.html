About the author
JR
J. R. Caldera
Archaeological thriller author
J.R. Caldera is a storyteller with a deep passion for ancient civilizations, forgotten knowledge, and the thrilling question of what happens when the past refuses to stay buried.

With a lifelong fascination for archaeology, hidden histories, and the shadowy lines where myth meets reality, he crafts fast-paced adventures filled with intelligent heroes, dangerous secrets, and twists that keep readers turning pages late into the night.

The Pyramid Protocol series is his heartfelt invitation to anyone who has ever looked at the pyramids and wondered: What if there's more? Writing these books has been a joyful journey of research, imagination, and pure excitement — and he hopes you'll feel that same sense of wonder while reading them.

When he's not writing, J.R. enjoys exploring old maps, learning about lost technologies, and imagining what secrets our ancestors might still be trying to tell us. He loves connecting with readers and always appreciates hearing which book or twist you enjoyed most.
